CartFly

Policy

Order Confirmation & Payment

All orders must be paid online in advance to confirm processing. Orders will only be prepared and shipped after payment confirmation has been successfully completed.

Refund & Return Policy

CartFly does not accept product exchanges or returns once an order has been placed.

Refunds are only eligible in cases where a product is proven to be damaged due to handling by CartFly during processing or shipping preparation.

Once an order is successfully placed and confirmed, refund requests will not be accepted, except under the condition stated above.

Shipping Cost Calculator

CartFly provides an integrated calculator service that allows users to estimate the expected shipping cost before placing an order.

The calculator provides an estimated payment amount based on available shipping details; however, final charges may vary depending on shipment specifications, destination requirements, or third-party delivery adjustments.

Real-Time Order Tracking

Customers can monitor and track their orders at every stage through CartFly’s real-time tracking system available within the application.

Delivery Options

Customers may choose one of the following delivery methods during the ordering process:

Locker Pickup

Customers can select their preferred CartFly locker location through the application and collect their order directly.

Home Delivery

Home delivery is provided through an independent third-party delivery agency. Once CartFly transfers the order to the delivery provider, all delivery coordination, communication, updates, and final delivery responsibility become the responsibility of that agency.
