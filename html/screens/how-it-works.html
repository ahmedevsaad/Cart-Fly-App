How Cartfly works?

1. Create your account

Sign up and set up your CartFly account in seconds. Once you join, you’ll get access to our warehouse addresses in multiple countries including the UAE, Saudi Arabia, Egypt, USA, and China.

2. Shop from your favourite websites

Choose any online store located in one of our warehouse countries.
For example, you can shop from Sephora UAE, Adidas USA, or any brand you love.

Add the products you want to the cart directly on the store’s website and proceed to the checkout page.

3. Use the CartFly checkout popup

When you reach the checkout page, the CartFly popup will appear with two options:

• Copy Warehouse Address
Copy your CartFly warehouse address and paste it directly into the shipping address field on the website to complete your purchase.

• Cart Calculator (optional)
Want to estimate costs before ordering?
Use the Cart Calculator to estimate customs, taxes, and delivery fees based on the number of items and packages.

4. Confirm your order

After completing the purchase on the store’s website, go back to CartFly and open the Order Confirmation section.
Enter your order number, name, and phone number.
Your order will automatically appear in your account as Pending.

5. Warehouse arrival & final payment

Once your package arrives at our warehouse, you will receive a notification.
We will calculate the exact customs, taxes, and shipping fees.
You can then complete the payment and get your order delivered to your doorstep.
